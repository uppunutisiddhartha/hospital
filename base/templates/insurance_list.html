Cashless Claim Procedure

Follow these 4 simple steps for seamless cashless hospitalization.

STEP 1: Intimation & Pre-Authorization

Contact the Insurance Desk upon admission. For planned surgery, notify us **3-5 days** prior.

STEP 2: Documentation Submission

Provide your **Health Card/Policy copy** and **Photo ID**. Our team submits the pre-auth request to the TPA.

STEP 3: TPA Approval

The TPA/Insurer reviews and issues a **final approval** letter covering the admissible treatment cost.

STEP 4: Discharge & Settlement

Upon discharge, the hospital settles the approved amount directly with the TPA. Patient pays the Non-admissible balance.

Reimbursement Claims

For non-cashless cases, patients can submit reimbursement claims post-treatment. Our Insurance Desk assists with the necessary documentation and claim submission process to ensure a smooth experience.

STEP 1: Collect Documents

Gather all medical documents, bills, prescriptions, and discharge summary from the hospital.

STEP 2: Claim Form

Obtain the reimbursement claim form from your insurance provider or TPA.

STEP 3: Submit Claim

Submit the completed claim form along with all supporting documents to your insurer/TPA.

STEP 4: Follow Up

Regularly follow up with your insurer/TPA for claim status and any additional requirements.
